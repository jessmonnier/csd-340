Chapter Title: About Me

I am a 36-year-old woman from many places, but mainly small-town Florida and the DC metro area. My father was in the Army while I was growing up, so I moved around numerous times, which is why it's a little difficult to pin down "home." I went to college to be a math teacher, but found it too difficult to manage a classroom, so I ended up joining the military. At first I recruited with the Army, but my dad told me that it would be better to join the Air Force, and ultimately that's what I did.

In the Air Force, I trained to become an airborne Korean linguist. I met my husband because we (entirely coincidentally) joined on the same day, went through basic training at the same time, and both ended up going to Monterey, California, to learn a language for our airborne linguist career field, although he learned Arabic instead of Korean. We dated throughout the 1.5-year language school, experienced six months of long distance when I moved on to our next training location before he did, and ended up getting married just a week or two before he moved on to that training location. We've been married for over six years now.

More recently, my husband and I were both selected to retrain into the Cyber Warfare career field within the Air Force, so that is what we've been doing. It is another long training pipeline; we've been at it for about a year and a half now and still have almost a full year left if all goes smoothly. On a more personal level, we have two German Shepherds and an unreasonably large board game collection. I like to read and go indoor bouldering. When all my creative energy isn't eaten up by programming and cyber-related pursuits, I also like to write.

Although the retrain into Cyber Warfare and the pursuit of this degree program are relatively recent developments in my life, I have been interested in this sort of thing since I learned basic HTML and CSS from the Neopets pet pages when I was in middle school. I have created multiple blog-style personal websites over the years, and also created and administered several online communities. I'm pretty good at googling my way to most things I want to do with a website, but it will be nice to tackle it in a more methodical way through this course.
